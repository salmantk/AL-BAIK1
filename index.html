OUR STORY






Welcome to Al-Baik: A Legacy of Flavor and Tradition
In 1974, our founder, Sh. Ragab Abdulkhaleq, had a vision to bring the authentic taste of the Middle East to the world. With a passion for cooking and a commitment to quality, he opened the first Al-Baik Restaurant in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia.
